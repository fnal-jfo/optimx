E-Electrostatic Accelerating Section

Parameter Description Argument Order Argument Name Units Default Value
Length 1 L cm 0.0
Energy Gain 2 Delta_E MeV 0.0

The letters "E" and "e" denote a section with electrostatic acceleration in the uniform electrostatic field. The first argument is the total length of the accelerating field. The second argument is the total energy change in the section. The program takes into account the effect of the beam focusing and defocusing at the entrance and exit of the section but does not include any beam space charge effect. 

Example: 
Eacc          	L[cm]=0   	Delta_E[MeV]=20
